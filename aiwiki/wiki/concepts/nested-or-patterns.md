# Nested Or-Patterns

### From: SIMP_RAGENT

A Rust syntax optimization where nested alternatives are grouped within a single pattern: Some('docx' | 'xlsx' | 'pptx') instead of Some('docx') | Some('xlsx') | Some('pptx')
